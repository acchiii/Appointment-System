📚 Enrollment Process

1. Submit required documents to the Registrar Office

  • Report Card or SHS First Semester Report Card
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • Copy of PSA
  • 4pcs. 2x2 ID picture with white background
  • ₱500.00 Enrollment Fee
  • Certificate of Scholarship (if available)

2. Pay enrollment fees at the cashier.

3. Get class schedule from EDP (no appointment needed, self-service).

🔄 Course Shifting

Contact academic advisor during enrollment period. Book Registrar appointment for form and approval if needed.
